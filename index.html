🎯 Phản xạ Phòng thi Part 2 (150 từ ~ 1.5 - 2 phút)

Intro: Paraphrase Đề bài trực tiếp ➜ Body: Triển khai 4 ô vuông phản xạ độc lập (Không nhìn bài mẫu) ➜ Conclusion: Chốt hạ cảm xúc

📌 Đề gốc áp dụng chung ý tưởng Thống Nhất Park:

  • • Describe a natural place / Describe a quiet place you like to go
  • • Describe a place you went to and an outdoor activity you did there
  • • Describe a place you would like to visit in your free time
  • • Describe a boring place (Biến tấu: Người khác chê buồn chán vì không có công nghệ, nhưng mình lại thích vì nó yên tĩnh)
Đề mẫu: Describe a quiet / natural place you like to go to
Target Band: 7.0+ | Topic đặc trị phản xạ tự nhiên
1. Where the place is & How you know it
  • Thong Nhat Park – suburb of Hanoi (capital of VN)
  • First knew it via best friend's camping birthday party
  • Left a strong impression on me
  • Frequency: Go there once/twice a month (weekends)
2. What it looks like (Atmosphere)
  • Picturesque and tranquil place
  • Full of lush trees, vibrant flowers, green spaces
  • Fresh air & slow-paced life (people walking/relaxing)
  • See friendly wildlife (squirrels, birds, cats)
3. What you do there (Outdoor activities)
  • Set up a small tent, sit on the grass, chat, take photos
  • Read a peaceful book, listen to acoustic music
  • Simply watch people and friendly animals around
4. Why you love it & Conclusion
  • Escape from the hustle and bustle of city life
  • Allows me to unwind, clear my mind & recharge energy
  • A quiet place that I truly treasure & highly recommend

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"I’d like to talk about a quiet natural place that I really enjoy going to, which is Thong Nhat Park. This park is located in the suburb of Hanoi, and I first knew about it when my best friend invited me for a small camping trip on her birthday. Since then, it has become my favorite spot to visit in my free time.

Thong Nhat Park is a picturesque and tranquil area. It is full of lush trees, flowers, and open green spaces, which make the atmosphere incredibly fresh. People there seem to lead a slow-paced life, walking, exercising, or simply relaxing. What I find most special is that you can spot many friendly animals like squirrels and birds, making the scenery even more peaceful and lively.

Whenever I visit, especially on weekends, I love doing outdoor activities like setting up a tent, sitting on the grass to read a book, or just enjoying the fresh air. This place allows me to escape from the hustle and bustle of city life and clear my mind. Overall, it is a peaceful haven that I truly treasure, and I always recommend it to anyone looking to unwind."

📌 Đề gốc áp dụng chung ý tưởng Bestie's House:

  • • Describe the home of someone you know well and that you often visit
  • • Describe a home that you like to visit but do not want to live in (Biến tấu: Do nhà nhiều đồ decor dễ vỡ hoặc quá hồng, không hợp style sống lâu dài của mình)
  • • Describe a place where you saw many animals (Biến tấu: Nhà bạn thân có bộ sưu tập thú nuôi độc đáo/nhiều chó mèo)
Đề mẫu: Describe the home of someone you know well and often visit
Target Band: 7.0+ | Tải từ vựng tả nội thất ấm cúng
1. Whose house it is & How often you visit
  • My best friend’s house – visit once or twice a month
  • Go there when we have free time to study or relax together
2. Exterior & Interior Style
  • Decorated in a minimalist style (mainly pink and white)
  • Reflects her cute and lovely personality
  • Extremely tidy, clean, and well-organized
3. Highlights inside the house (Books/Animals)
  • Basic furniture: TV, small table, princess-style bed
  • Large collection of cute stuffed animals & real pets (cats/puppies)
  • Huge bookshelf filled with various novels and English textbooks
4. Your feelings & Conclusion
  • Borrowing books helps broaden my horizons
  • Feels warm, cozy, and highly comfortable
  • Hope to own a similar home in the future

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"Today, I’d like to describe the home of someone I know very well and often visit, which is my best friend’s house. I usually go there a few times a month, especially when we want to study or unwind together over the weekend.

Her home is beautifully decorated in a minimalist style, mainly with pastel pink and white colors, which perfectly reflects her lovely personality. The house is always spotlessly clean and tidy, with everything neatly arranged. What impresses me the most is her room, which features a massive bookshelf filled with various novels, detective stories, and English materials. I really enjoy borrowing books from her because it helps broaden my horizons.

Additionally, her house is like a mini sanctuary where she keeps a large collection of cute stuffed animals and even some friendly pet cats. It is such a warm and cozy place that makes me feel completely at ease. Although I love visiting it to relax, I wouldn't want to live there permanently because the delicate decor requires too much careful maintenance. Overall, it's a wonderful place that I always look forward to visiting."

📌 Đề gốc áp dụng chung ý tưởng Clothes Store:

  • • Describe a shop/store you enjoy visiting
  • • Describe a place where you received excellent customer service
Đề mẫu: Describe a shop/store you enjoy visiting (Excellent Service)
Target Band: 7.0+ | Từ vựng chủ đề mua sắm và dịch vụ hành vi
1. Where the shop is & How often you go
  • A local clothing boutique near my house (vibrant shopping area)
  • Very convenient to access
  • Visit once every few months (special occasions like Tet holiday)
2. What the shop looks like
  • Beautiful pastel tone decoration, cozy lightings
  • Well-arranged clothing racks, easy to find items
3. Why you enjoy visiting (Customer Service)
  • Excellent customer service – staff are friendly, polite, helpful
  • Patiently suggest clothes that suit my body shape and style
  • Specific example: Patiently looked for the perfect shoe size without rushing me
4. Your feelings & Conclusion
  • Makes me feel comfortable, valued, and satisfied
  • A retail shop that I would highly recommend to my family

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"I’d like to talk about a shop that I really enjoy visiting, which is a local clothing boutique located in a busy shopping area not far from my house. Because of its convenient location, I usually drop by once every few months, especially when I need to shop for important occasions like the Tet holiday.

The store is beautifully designed with soft pastel colors and warm lighting, creating a very welcoming atmosphere. However, the main reason I keep coming back is their excellent customer service. The staff members are incredibly polite, professional, and attentive. They always take the time to listen to my preferences and patiently suggest outfits that perfectly suit my body shape.

I remember a time when I was struggling to find the right shoe size. The sales assistant didn't show any annoyance; instead, she didn't rush me at all and went back to the stockroom multiple times until she found the perfect fit. This level of dedication always leaves a positive impression on me. Shopping there always makes me feel comfortable and completely satisfied, which is why I constantly recommend it to my friends."

📌 Đề gốc áp dụng chung ý tưởng Mỹ Đình Stadium:

  • • Describe a popular place for sports that you’ve been to
  • • Describe a city you enjoyed visiting / a place you travelled to to recommend
  • • Describe an important river/lake/landmark in your country (Cận biên biến tấu không gian rộng lớn)
Đề mẫu: Describe a popular place for sports / city place you enjoyed visiting
Target Band: 7.0+ | Trải nghiệm dịch thuật / tình nguyện viên chuyên nghiệp
1. What and where the place is
  • My Dinh National Stadium – central part of Hanoi
  • Visited a few years ago as a volunteer during SEA Games
2. What you did there (Activities)
  • Supported football players, teams, and coaches
  • Arranged resting areas, prepared meals/drinks, behind-the-scenes tasks
  • Cheered passionately during intense matches
3. A memorable highlight (Interpreter)
  • Worked as a live interpreter between coaches
  • Challenging but extremely rewarding task
  • Boosted my self-confidence immensely
4. Your feelings & Conclusion
  • The atmosphere was incredibly energetic and exciting
  • An unforgettable landmark experience that made me proud

📝 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh (Sample Answer):

"Today, I'd like to describe a popular landmark and sports venue that I enjoyed visiting, which is My Dinh Stadium, located in Hanoi. I went there a few years ago when I had the precious opportunity to work as a member of a volunteer group during the SEA Games event.

During the tournament, I participated in various behind-the-scenes activities to support the international football players and their coaching staff. My duties included organizing logistics, arranging resting areas, and preparing meals. What made this experience especially memorable was that I got to challenge myself as a live interpreter, facilitating communication between foreign and local coaches.

Although the task was quite demanding, it was highly rewarding and made me feel proud of myself. The overall atmosphere at the stadium was always incredibly energetic and exciting with thousands of fans cheering. Visiting this place helped me gain immense confidence and valuable life experience. It is definitely an amazing place that I would highly recommend to anyone visiting the capital city."
